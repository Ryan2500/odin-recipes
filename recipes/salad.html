Spinach and Strawberry Salad

We love this spinach strawberry salad all year round if we can find stawberries!

This salad will contain loads of delicious ingredients, including spinach and strawberries.
It will be great any time strawberries are in season.

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Strawberries
  • Vegetable oil
  • White sugar
  • White wine vinegar
  • sesame seeds
  • poppy seeds
  • paprika

Steps

  1. Toss spinach and strawberries together in a large bowl
  2. Whisk oil, sugar, vinegar, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, and paprika together in a medium bowl;
    pour over spinach and strawberries, and toss to coat
